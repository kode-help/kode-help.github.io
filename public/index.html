Our Objectives

We want to make a big impact in a big sector.

A Connected Environment

We want to make a Community of developers to share each others knowlegde about programming.

Curated and Professional

A community where good quality is encouraged, and bad quality is removed.

Provide a Gateway to Programming

Create a environment where it is easy for beginners to enter, and familiarize themselves with key ideas in computer science.

Connect with Non-Profits

Allow new developers to connect with non-profits and gain experience. While still allowing non-profits to get the help they need.

Create Passion in the Community

Develop a sense of understanding and love for other developers in the community, and encourage helping new comers.

Multi-platform and accesible

We want to make this platform easily accesible to everyone.
